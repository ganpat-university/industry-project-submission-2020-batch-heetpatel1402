Phishing Information

Phishing is a type of cyber attack where attackers use deceptive emails, websites, or other means to trick individuals into providing sensitive information such as usernames, passwords, or financial details. To protect yourself from phishing:

  • Be cautious about clicking on links or downloading attachments from unknown sources.
  • Verify the legitimacy of emails, especially those requesting sensitive information.
  • Use strong, unique passwords and enable two-factor authentication where possible.
  • Keep your software and security tools up to date to protect against known vulnerabilities.
